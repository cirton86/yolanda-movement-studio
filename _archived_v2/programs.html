Training Programs

Personalized coaching designed for your body, your history, and your goals. Choose the path that fits your life stage.

Longevity Strength Training

For adults 40+ who want functional strength for daily life.

Who This Program Is For

  • Adults 40+ who want functional strength for daily life
  • People managing chronic pain (low back, knees, shoulders)
  • Those who feel intimidated by traditional gym environments
  • Anyone prioritizing long-term health over short-term intensity

What We Do Together

1
Assessment Phase (Weeks 1-2)

Movement screening, identify limitations, establish baseline strength.

2
Foundation Building (Weeks 3-8)

Master fundamental movement patterns, build body awareness.

Rebuild & Return (Post-PT)

Bridge the gap between physical therapy and independent movement.

Who This Program Is For

  • Recently discharged from PT but not feeling fully confident
  • Cleared to exercise but unsure how to progress safely
  • Afraid of reinjury or setbacks

Performance Prep

For athletes training for specific events (HYROX, Half-Marathon, etc).

Who This Program Is For

  • Training for HYROX, marathons, or obstacle course races
  • Climbers preparing for competitions
  • Endurance athletes wanting injury prevention
