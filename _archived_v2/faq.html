Getting Started

We start with a conversation about your history and goals. Then we move through a series of basic movement patterns (squat, hinge, reach) to assess mobility, stability, and strength. It is not a "workout" designed to make you sweat, but to gather data.

Clients often feel different (better posture, less stiffness) within 2-3 weeks. Observable strength gains typically take 6-8 weeks of consistent training.
